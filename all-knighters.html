The Team

All-Knighters -- a pun on the all-nighters we tend to pull before competitions -- is a team composed of Seniors and Freshmen. Many members are heavily involved in building the robot, programming the teleops and autons, and organizing outreach events. We live and breathe robotics; we love learning, designing, and seeing our ideas come into fruition.
