The Team

The Knightmares are a team composed of Juniors and Sophmores. We are a very integrated team, with many members being part of more than one subteam. Before FTC, some of our members had robotics experience in middle school. We all are incredibly passionate about robotics and always look to learn new things everyday. Like our sister team, we organize outreach events to get other (mostly middle schoolers) also interested in robotics.
