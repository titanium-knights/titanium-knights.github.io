All-Knighters

The All-Knighters are a team composed of seniors and freshmen. The seniors, having the most experience out of everyone on the team, simultaneously design, build, and code a robot with the freshmeen, while also teaching them the fundamentals of robotics.

Two Teams

Together, our two teams collaborate, sharing advice, resources, and experience, while also maintaining separate identities and design processes.

Knight-mares

The Knightmares are a team composed of juniors and sophmores. With most members having prior experience with robotics, all members are able to fully take part in the design process, and collaborate to build the robot.

How Our Team Works

Each team is split up into 3 different sections, build, programming, and outreach. Each section has their own responsibilities. It is common for members to be on more than one subteam.

  • Build
    This team is responsible for actually building the robot, the build teams creates CAD models of the robot and takes the time to assemble the robot together and fix flaws that may exist with the build
  • Programming
    This team is responsible for programming the robot, once the robot is built it must be able to react from user movement. The programmers use Java and kotlin to make the robot interactive and controllable by a phone
  • Outreach
    Building robots is expensive, the non-tech is team is responsible for outreach with other companies to gain funding to build the robots, and attend the competitions. They are also responsible for everything else that is not related to build or programming.
